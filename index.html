Our Services

We are Australia's for-purpose business intelligence firm

At daRta, we understand that a for-purpose business's most valuable asset is its people. Thus, the success of a business is determined by how its people spend their time.

Our mission is to empower you to spend less time in excel, and more time taking informed action based on reliable information.

About Us



Business Intelligence is no longer the domain of big business

Business intelligence - the art of transforming data into actionable information - can seem inaccessible to for-purpose businesses due to the time required to do it oneself, the costs associated with hiring people with data skills, and the price of software.

Yet every business, regardless of size, relies on information to make decisions and take informed action.

At daRta, our goal is to make this big business skillset accessible to the for-purpose sector. Let us handle your data, so you can spend your time where it really matters - running the business.
