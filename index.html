My Services

I am an Australian for-purpose business intelligence freelancer

For-purpose business' most valuable assets are their people. Thus, the success of a business - how much impact it can generate - is determined by how its people spend their time.

My mission is to empower you to spend less time in excel, and more time taking informed action based on reliable information.

About Me



Business Intelligence is no longer the domain of big business

Business intelligence - the art of transforming data into actionable information - can seem inaccessible to for-purpose businesses due to the time required to do it oneself, the costs associated with hiring people with data skills, and the price of software.

Yet every business, regardless of size, relies on information to make decisions and take informed action.

My goal is to make this big business skillset accessible to the for-purpose sector. Let me handle your data, so you can spend your time where it really matters - running the business.
