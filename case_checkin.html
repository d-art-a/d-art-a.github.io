Case Study

Check-in App

Problem

Imagine that your business runs drop-in events or workshops aimed at bringing the community together or improving knowledge. You’ve been running these events for a while now and have tracked attendance on paper. However, now that your events are becoming more popular, the paper-based system is proving difficult to maintain, and you want to get a better understanding of your community.

Solution

daRta could build a solution that would improve your check-in experience and allow you to track attendance. Imagine that, when a customer turns up to one of your drop-in events, they are greeted with a tablet device running a custom-built app. If this is their first event, they enter some basic details about themselves, otherwise they simply find their name to check in. This tablet is also available after the event for quick feedback of the customer’s experience. These data are instantly sent back to your business intellignece platform, where it is processed and fed into a report. In additional to the data collected, an AI algorithm is building a profile of your customers to help you better understand your target audience. Your new report is available on demand, allowing you to track attendance while you are checking people in, and analyse trends in your community over time after the event.

Result

You now have the ability to track KPIs, such as the number of new and returning customers attending each week, and the satisfaction with the content presented. As a result, you’ve been able to tailor both you event content and your marketing strategies to your target audience, improving engagement and increasing the number new clients attending your events.

Can you relate?

  • Do you collect data on paper, or manually enter it into excel?

  • Are you not collecting feedback from your events?

  • Do you struggle to track customer retention?

  • Is your target audience really who you think it is?
