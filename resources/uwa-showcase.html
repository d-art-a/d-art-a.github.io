UWA's Sustainable Campus Dashboard

In the above on-demand webinar, Jason showcases some of the work he completed for University of Western Australia's sustainability team. You can find the publicly available dashboard by clicking here.

During the webinar, Jason will:

  • Walk through and explain what sort of information you can get out of the public dashboard
  • Give you a sneak peak into some of UWA's internal PowerBI reports
  • Highlight how UWA is working towards a sustainable campus using data
  • Explain what the backend architecture looks like, and how the frontend was created
  • Share some tips and tricks he learned along the way
