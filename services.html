Business intelligence (BI) leverages a suite of tools to collect and transform data into information that informs a business’s strategic and operational decisions.

Outsource your BI team

Hire a team of data experts, at a fraction of the cost

“Business intelligence” is a term that has historically been the domain of big business. This is because a business intelligence platform requires a team of data experts to run it, and enough work to keep them busy year-round.

Hiring dARTa is the equivalent of hiring your own team of data experts, but with one big difference: you only pay for what you need, when you need it, both in terms of the technology, and the people.
